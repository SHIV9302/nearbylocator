Indori Food and Restaurants....

  Indore is a good city to visit.
  The food culture of Indore is fantastic.
  Poha Jalebi in Breakfast is very popular here in Indore.
  Indore is famous for Khopra Patties and Bhutte ka kees.
  Moong bhajia are also very famous.
